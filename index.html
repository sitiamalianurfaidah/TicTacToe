About the Game

Tic Tac Toe is a simple yet fun strategy game. Whether you play as X or O, your goal is to get three in a row!

How to Play

  • 🧠 Choose your player (X or O)
  • 👆 Click a square to make your move
  • 🏆 Get 3 in a row to win!
  • 🔁 Click Restart to play again
